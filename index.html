About

Yo, I'm a Digital Media Specialist with 7+ years of experience in creating and producing digital content. Everything from directing short form documentaries to editing podcasts, coiling SDI lines in the rain to writing news plugs, taking headshots and stop-motion to debugging scripts at unholy hours and most everything in between (no one has let me do their make-up yet).

Penchant for people, places, pictures, and the novel.
